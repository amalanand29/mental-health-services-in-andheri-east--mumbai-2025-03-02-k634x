OUR STORY

About OmniHealth

Founded in 2015, OmniHealth emerged from a vision to make quality mental health care accessible to everyone in Mumbai. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated professionals and has grown into a comprehensive mental health center serving thousands of clients.

Our mission is to provide evidence-based, compassionate mental health care that empowers individuals to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential. We believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms, and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professional care while keeping our services affordable and accessible.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and approach

What types of mental health conditions do you treat?

We provide comprehensive care for anxiety, depression, stress, relationship issues, trauma, and other mental health concerns. Our expert team uses evidence-based approaches tailored to your needs.

How long does each therapy session last?

Standard sessions are 50 minutes, while initial consultations are 90 minutes to allow for thorough assessment. We offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend appointments.

What are your payment options and fees?

Sessions start at ₹1000, with sliding scale options available. We accept major insurance plans and offer flexible payment arrangements to ensure accessibility.

Do you offer home counseling services?

Yes, we provide home counseling services for clients who have mobility challenges or prefer the comfort of their own environment. This service is available in Andheri East and surrounding areas in Mumbai.

Are your services confidential?

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality in accordance with professional ethics and legal requirements. Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.
